The Power of Micro LED: "The Wall"

Samsung's "The Wall" represents a paradigm shift in display technology. Leveraging self-lit RGB Micro LEDs, it delivers unparalleled visual fidelity, modularity, and performance, making it a transformative solution for broadcast, virtual production, and luxury commercial environments.

🔆

Pure Black & Infinite Contrast

Black Seal Technology and individually controlled pixels achieve perfect blacks and stunning depth, crucial for on-camera realism.

🌈

Ultra Chroma Color

20-bit processing delivers exceptionally pure and accurate RGB colors, vital for color-critical broadcast applications.

🧠

MICRO AI Processor

Instantly optimizes content up to 8K, enhancing contrast and removing noise for flawless picture quality.

📺

Flicker-Free Broadcast

Ultra-high refresh rates (up to 15,360Hz) and Genlock ensure seamless, artifact-free on-camera performance.

🧰

Modular Canvas

Limitless flexibility in size, shape, and curvature (up to 5800R/6000R), adapting to any creative vision or studio layout.

🛠

Effortless Installation

Factory Seam Adjustment and cable-less docking connections significantly reduce setup time and complexity.

"The Wall" is particularly impactful in virtual production, enabling creators to build immersive, dynamic sets that blend physical and digital worlds seamlessly. Its fine pixel pitches (down to 0.84mm) and advanced studio frame rate support make it a cornerstone of modern content creation.

Onyx Cinema LED: Immersive Experiences

Samsung Onyx redefines the cinematic and large-venue viewing experience. As the world's first DCI-certified Cinema LED display, Onyx delivers breathtaking visuals, making it ideal for movie theaters, live sports, concerts, and high-impact presentations.

🎞

DCI-Certified Brilliance

Adheres to rigorous Digital Cinema Initiatives standards, ensuring professional-grade performance and reliability.

🌙

True Black Immersion

Self-emissive LEDs produce true blacks and infinite contrast, eliminating washed-out visuals common with projectors.

🎭

High Frame Rate Action

Supports 4K resolution at 120Hz, delivering ultra-smooth motion for fast-paced sports, concerts, and gaming.

☀️

Bright Room Ready

Peak brightness of 300 nits (6x cinema standard) ensures vivid playback even in environments with ambient light.

📏

Flexible Scaling

Modular design allows for custom screen sizes from 5m to over 20m wide, fitting diverse venue dimensions.

👷

Long-Term Reliability

Industry-first 10-year warranty and auto-calibration ensure consistent performance and future-proof investment.

Onyx empowers venues to diversify their offerings beyond traditional cinema, hosting a wide range of live and alternative content with unparalleled visual impact, creating new revenue streams and engaging audiences like never before.
